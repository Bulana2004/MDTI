About us

Who Are We?

We MDTI is the Leading Training Institute in Sri Lanka

Established in year 2000, ICBT is the leading tertiary education providers in Sri Lanka with the largest number of students in the private sector higher education industry. Popularly known as ICBT Campus, ICBT offers Certificate level programs, Diplomas, Higher Diplomas, Pre-University foundation programs, Undergraduate programs and Masters level programs in association with leading Universities and educational service providers in UK and Australia.
