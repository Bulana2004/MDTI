About us

Who Are We?

We MDTI is the Leading Training Institute in Sri Lanka

Established in year 2000, MDTI is the leading tertiary education providers in Sri Lanka with the largest number of students in the private sector higher education industry. Popularly known as ICBT Campus, ICBT offers Certificate level programs, Diplomas, Higher Diplomas, Pre-University foundation programs, Undergraduate programs and Masters level programs in association with leading Universities and educational service providers in UK and Australia.

Our Vision

Our Mission

To contribute to the sustainable development of the Southern Province by enhancing human resources based on the needs of public, private, service, and institutional sectors, through the provision of high-quality training and consultancy services, and the introduction of management development methodologies, in order to create a prosperous Sri Lanka.
